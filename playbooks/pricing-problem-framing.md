# Framing pricing problems

## Start with the outcome delta

1. What moved? (GRS, margin, lift, discount cost)
2. What caused it? (mechanism in one sentence)
3. What do we do next? (recommendation)

## Common pricing PM mistakes

- Multiplying status-quo conversion × volume × ASP and calling it "incremental GP"
- Treating advertised discount rate as realized cost (conditional offers vs WSC)
- Shipping science progress reports instead of decision-ready narratives

## My default framing template

```
[Metric] moved X% because [intervention].
Recommend: [action] by [date/scope].
Evidence: [one chart or experiment ref].
```
